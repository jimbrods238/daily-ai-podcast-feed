    ## Episode Summary


    Three AI releases today — Qwen3.7-Plus, MiniMax M3, and Mellum2 — independently converged on the same architectural premise: that the unit of AI value has shifted from single-inference to multi-step agentic loops. The simultaneous adoption of sparsity-based architectures (MoE and sparse attention) across unrelated labs signals an engineering consensus, not a coincidence. The episode argues that the real competitive story with Chinese lab releases is fast productization of proven Western research paired with platform lock-in plays — a more nuanced threat than simple capability catch-up.
