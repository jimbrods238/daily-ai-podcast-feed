    # When Washington Overruled the Safety Playbook


    Anthropic built a powerful coding model, judged it safe enough to release, and published it — then the U.S. government slapped export controls on it within days, with no institutional process to resolve the disagreement. Today's episode argues that the entire responsible-AI framework was designed for a world where labs and governments roughly agreed on what 'safe' means, and the Anthropic-Mythos standoff is the first public proof that they don't.
